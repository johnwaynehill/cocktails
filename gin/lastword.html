    Ingredients

    • 3/4 oz Gin
    • 3/4 oz Green Chartreuse
    • 3/4 oz Maraschino Liqueur
    • 3/4 oz Lime Juice

    Directions

    Add all ingredients to shaker. Add ice. Shake for 10-20 seconds until very cold. Double strain into chilled rocks glass.
