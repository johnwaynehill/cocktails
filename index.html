    Cocktail of the Month

    Ingredients

    • 3 oz Whiskey
    • 1 tbsp Sugar
    • 3 Sprigs of Spearmint

    Directions

    Add 2 sprigs worth of mint leaves to glass. Add sugar. Add 1/4 oz water, or a bit of the whiskey. Muddle the mint lightly to release the essential oils and aromas. Add whiskey. Pack cup with crushed ice. Add remaining sprig of mint for garnish.
